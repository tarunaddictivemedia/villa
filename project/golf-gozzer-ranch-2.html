Golf Course at Gozzer Ranch

Overview

At most Discovery clubs, golf is one of the anchor amenities. At Discovery Builders we took a meticulous approach in overseeing the construction of this 18-hole Tom Fazio designed course, working closely together to ensure that the course was built to the highest standards of quality.

The process

Throughout the design and construction process, we placed a strong emphasis on environmental sustainability, working to minimize the course's impact on the local environment and using eco-friendly materials and construction practices whenever possible.
